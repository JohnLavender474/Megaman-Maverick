# Saw

- **Layer:** `hazards`
- **Canonical size:** 32 × 32 px (also seen at 32×24)
- **Observed `spawn_type`:** `spawn_room` (typical)
- **Known properties:**
  - `type` — movement direction (e.g. `"R"` for right)
  - `spawn_room` — required when `spawn_type=spawn_room`

Moving circular saw blade. Travels along a path. Good for narrow corridors where the
player must time their movement.

Source: observed across multiple levels.
