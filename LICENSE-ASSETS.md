## Origin of assets

The assets in this repository (sprites, tilesets, music, sound effects, fonts,
etc.) originate from one of the following sources:

1. **Direct contributions from community creators.** Artists, musicians, and
   designers credited in `assets/credits.txt` and `README.md` provided their
   work directly to this project. Each such asset remains the property of its
   original creator and is included here with their permission for use within
   this project.

2. **Publicly distributed third-party assets.** Some assets were obtained from
   public sources where they were offered for free download or for sale (asset
   packs, community sprite sheets, royalty-free sound libraries, etc.). These
   remain subject to whatever license or terms of use the original creator
   attached to them at the point of distribution.

3. **Material derived from official Capcom Mega Man titles.** Sprites, sound
   effects, music, and likenesses originating from Capcom games are the
   intellectual property of Capcom Co., Ltd. See `NOTICE.md`.

## What this means for reuse

**The maintainer of this project cannot grant you a license to any asset in
this repository.** If you want to reuse an asset from `assets/`, you must:

- Identify the original creator (start with `assets/credits.txt` and the
  README credits section).
- Locate the original distribution and license terms for that asset.
- Obtain permission from, or comply with the terms set by, the original
  creator.

Permission granted to *this* project does **not** transfer to forks or
downstream users. A contributor who shared sprites with this project did not
necessarily authorize redistribution by third parties.

## For contributors and creators

If you are a creator whose work appears in this repository and you wish to:

- have your work removed,
- have your attribution corrected or expanded, or
- have terms of use clarified,

please open an issue on the project repository or contact the maintainer
directly. Removal or re-attribution requests will be honored promptly.
